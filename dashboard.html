Add PulseLink in 30 Seconds
1
Add the relay flag to your mev-boost client
Append PulseLink to your existing -relays list, or use it standalone:
mev-boost -relays https://pulselinkrelay.org
2
Verify the connection
mev-boost will call GET /eth/v1/builder/status on startup. You should see 200 OK. Your validator registration is automatic when your beacon client connects.
3
Done — you're live
Builders will begin submitting bids for your slots. The relay stores the highest-value bid per slot and delivers the full payload when you request it.
About PulseLink
PulseLink is an open-source MEV-Boost relay on Base mainnet. It acts as a trusted intermediary between block builders and validators, ensuring blocks are fairly auctioned and efficiently delivered to the network.
Add https://pulselinkrelay.org to your MEV-Boost client's -relays flag. PulseLink is compatible with all standard MEV-Boost implementations including Flashbots' mev-boost client.
PulseLink Relay is independently operated. The designated fee recipient address is 0x94b98bce7c82C2dF26966D125575f2376Ac57352. All fees collected from submitted blocks are forwarded to this address.
Yes. PulseLink Relay is completely free for validators. There are no subscription fees, registration requirements, or minimum stake thresholds.
POST your block payload to https://pulselinkrelay.org/relay/v1/builder/blocks with a JSON body containing block_number, block_hash, gas_fee, and builder_id. Blocks with missing fields or zero fees are rejected.
